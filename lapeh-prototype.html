LapehDelivery Dispatch Platform
MVP product design · UI prototype

Restaurants get a driver on demand — in a few taps.

Lapeh isn't an ordering marketplace. Restaurants take orders their own way, then use one role-aware app to dispatch the nearest driver, while customers confirm location and pay from a simple web link. Below is the full screen-by-screen flow for each role.

Restaurant app

Linked · The customer

Customer web page

No app to install. The customer taps an SMS / WhatsApp link to pin their location, pay, and track — all in the browser.

Role 02 · The driver

Driver app

Go online, accept the closest request, navigate, and confirm hand-off with a delivery code.

Lapeh — one Flutter app, two roles (restaurant & driver), plus a lightweight customer web link. Designed for a Laravel + MySQL backend with Google Maps navigation and push notifications. This is a visual prototype for client review; copy, pricing (AED 7 base + AED 1.5/km) and content are placeholders and fully configurable from the admin panel.
